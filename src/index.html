What you need to do to test this?

  1. Get 2 devices and open this website on them. (2 Browsers on the same device will throw an error.)
  2. Click on 'Join the room' from the one device.
  3. Click on 'Join the room' from the other device.
  4. Wait some seconds until they connect successfully.

    5. Note that the call will finish after 1 minute. Enjoy!
